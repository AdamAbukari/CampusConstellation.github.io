# Campus Constellation  
*The University Ecosystem Project*

**Mission Statement**  
"To connect every university student with everything they need on campus through a single, student-powered ecosystem that saves time, money, and builds community."

### Three Core Principles
1. **Student-First Design** ‚Äî Built by students, for students  
2. **Community Economics** ‚Äî Value circulates within the student community  
3. **Zero-Friction Onboarding** ‚Äî No barriers to entry for users or student businesses

---

## I. Business Model Architecture

### Three-Pillar System
1. **Information Hub** (Free Layer)  
   - Verified hostel/dorm listings with student reviews  
   - Campus maps with real-time facility status  
   - Event calendars and club directories  
   - Campus survival guides (by seniors for freshmen)

2. **Student Commerce Platform** (Zero-Commission Launch Phase)  
   - **CampusStore**: Student business storefronts  
   - **Textbook Galaxy**: Peer-to-peer textbook marketplace  
   - **SkillSwap**: Services marketplace (tutoring, design, coding)  
   - **Dorm Delivery**: Student-run delivery network

3. **Campus Integration Layer**  
   - University partnership portal  
   - Verified local business listings (post-validation)  
   - Campus job board

### Unique Economic Model
- **Phase 1** (6 months): 100% free, zero commissions  
- **Phase 2**: 5% commission only on transactions >$50  
- **Phase 3**: Premium features for businesses ($5‚Äì10/month)  
- **Sustainability**: Campus ambassador programs, sponsored content spaces

## II. Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1‚Äì4)
**Tools**: AppCreator24, Bubble.io, or FlutterFlow  
**Features**:
- User profiles with .edu email verification
- Hostel database with filters (price, distance, amenities)
- Simple marketplace with 5 categories: Textbooks, Electronics, Dorm supplies, Services, Event tickets

### Phase 2: Activation (Weeks 5‚Äì8)
- In-app messaging system
- Review and rating system
- Campus delivery request board
- Basic search functionality

### Phase 3: Growth (Weeks 9‚Äì12)
- Group buying functionality
- Campus announcement system
- Study group matching
- Simple payment integration (Venmo/UPI API)

---

## III. Testing & Launch Strategy

### Beta Testing Approach
1. Start micro: Single university, one dorm building  
2. ‚ÄúDorm Zero‚Äù pilot with 50 students  
3. Daily feedback loops  
4. Ambassador program: 10 students per faculty

### Free Tools Stack
- **Prototyping**: Figma (free for students)  
- **No-Code**: Bubble.io (student plan), Glide, AppCreator24  
- **Backend**: Firebase or Supabase (generous free tiers)  
- **Communication**: Discord, Telegram/WhatsApp groups

### Validation Metrics
- Activation: 30% of dorm students use within 2 weeks  
- Retention: 3+ uses/week per active user  
- Transaction success: 80% of listings get responses  
- Growth: 10% weekly organic growth via referrals
